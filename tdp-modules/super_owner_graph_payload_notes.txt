Super owner graph patch

Core change:
A payload is no longer promoted to a super because it is large damage, projectile-ish, or fmt=super_like.
A payload is only promoted when it is owned by a super-proven action graph.

Required proof chain:
1. 00/23 dispatch row found.
2. Dispatch child link resolves.
3. Selector maps to a move ID in the super band, such as 0x160/0x161/0x170.
4. Super marker exists on that move graph, such as meter, SuperBG, kind super/hyper, or move-map super label.
5. Payload is reached/owned by the graph, either:
   - parent -> resolved child -> 35/10 script-hit command, or
   - same post-link / root graph as the super move entry, or
   - payload name/family matches the proven super entry.

Result:
- Grand Slam / Homerun 1360 payload becomes script_hit_payload owned by super_graph.
- Mound Blast remains payload_only unless its own parent graph proves it.
- Shinkuu beam card can still attach when the Shinkuu selector/name/meter/SuperBG proves ownership.
- Morrigan missile payload stays attached through the known Finishing Shower graph/payload relationship.

New UI fields:
- Confidence
- Super Proof
- Owner Proof
- Owned Payload Summary
- Payload-Only Scout

Important terminology:
format = how bytes are laid out, such as script_hit_payload, beam_card, projectile_template.
owner = who owns the payload, such as super_graph or payload_only.
